Who Are We?

Our Story: Summer Music Air

Launched in 2023 with the vision to redefine the Irish music scene, Summer Music Air is Dublin’s newest and most electrifying annual music festival. We were founded on the simple idea that summer nights in Ireland deserve a soundtrack of unforgettable energy, world-class performers, and community spirit.

What We Stand For:

Dublin’s Energy: Born and based in the heart of Dublin, we embrace the city’s legendary hospitality, culture, and buzz to create an immersive festival experience unlike any other.

A Curated Soundtrack: We carefully hand-pick a diverse lineup that spans global headliners and the best emerging Irish talemt, ensuring every beat and stage offers a discovery.

The Ultimate Summer Destination: More than just music, we are a destination where friends meet, memories are made, and the spirit of summer comes alive under the stars.

Come join us to be part of Dublin’s next great music legacy.
